About me

I'm Harsh and I'm a

As a teenager at school, I was the kid other students approached about issues with their phone or laptop. I was the go-to guy for fixing resolving any technical problems. Today, not much has changed. I’m still the first point of call for technical issues. The difference is I now also develop innovative software, mobile applications, voice assistance application, web application to solve the problems. My natural curiosity for computers is what drove me towards my computer science degree. I've always been fascinated by computers and the technical mechanics behind the monitor. In my spare time, I manage to run my podcast show. I love to talk with people from diverse backgrounds, understanding their visions, learning from their experiences, and creating value for each other is now my hobby. I believe by sharing, we begin to link, and by linking, we begin to connect, and by connecting, we begin to heal. My biggest motivation? Just to keep challenging myself. I see life almost like one long University education -- every day I'm learning something new. I am super excited about entrepreneurship, I believe entrepreneurs have the power to shape the world by developing versatile skill sets and profitable businesses that are crucial to the progress of our economies. I have been always very impressed and fascinated by the quote of Sir Steve Jobs - "Your time is limited, so don’t waste it living someone else’s life.
